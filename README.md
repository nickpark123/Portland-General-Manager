This is the Portland General Manager website, a place where you can act as the GM of the Portland Trail Blazers, a renowned and widely successful NBA Team. 
You can select up to 15 players from a pool of players who have or currently play for the Blazers. The only limitation is that you must obey the salary cap 
of $154,647,000.
